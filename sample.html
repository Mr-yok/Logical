About PRIST University - Madurai Campus


PRIST University (Ponnaiyah Ramajayam Institute of Science and Technology) is a well-established institution that offers a wide range of academic programs.

The Madurai Campus is one of its regional centers, focused on delivering quality education and industry-relevant training in Southern Tamil Nadu.

Located in the temple city of Madurai, the campus offers a peaceful and academically enriching environment. It provides courses in engineering, management, arts, and science disciplines.

The campus is equipped with modern classrooms, labs, libraries, and placement support services to ensure students receive both theoretical and practical knowledge for their career growth.
